CLASS TYPES

Term Time Junior Classes

Tiny Tennis (p1 & 2)

Our ‘Tiny Tennis’ classes help young children to build coordination skills through numerous games and ‘racket & ball’ exercises. They last 45 minutes each and are suitable for children in P1 & P2.

Mini Tennis Beginners (p3 – 5)

Mini tennis uses small courts and ‘low compression’ balls to make the game easier for children to learn. Lessons are kept dynamic with numerous games and drills that help children develop the proper technique for all the main strokes – forehand, backhand, serve & volleys.

Each class lasts one hour and the ‘Mini Tennis Beginners’ class is suitable for children from P3 to P5 who are learning the initial skills.

Mini Tennis Improvers (p3 – 5)

Mini tennis uses small courts and ‘low compression’ balls to make the game easier for children to learn. Lessons are kept dynamic with numerous games and drills that help children develop the proper technique for all the main strokes – forehand, backhand, serve & volleys.

Each class lasts one hour and the ‘Mini Tennis Improvers’ class is suitable for children from P3 to P5 who have been to classes before and are continuing to develop the skills necessary to play matches.

Mini Tennis Advanced (p3 – 5)

‘Mini Tennis Advanced’ classes continue to use the scaled down mini tennis court in order to allow children to play matches using the same tactics as adult players use on a full size court. Players are invited to join an advanced class once they have mastered all the strokes.

Mid Tennis Beginners (p6 – F2)

Mid tennis classes (or mini tennis orange) allow children to progress to the full size net while using a modified court layout and low compression balls to make the game easier.

Lessons are kept dynamic with numerous games while teaching children the proper technique for all the main strokes in tennis – forehand, backhand, serve & volleys.

Each class lasts one hour and the ‘Mid Tennis Beginners’ class is suitable for children in P6-F2 who are learning the initial skills.

Mid Tennis Improvers (p6 – F2)

Mid tennis classes (or mini tennis orange) allow children to progress to the full size net while using a modified court layout and low compression balls to make the game easier.

Lessons are kept dynamic with numerous games while teaching children the proper technique for all the main strokes in tennis – forehand, backhand, serve & volleys.

Each class lasts one hour and the ‘Mid Tennis Improvers’ class is suitable for children in P6-F2 who have been to classes before and are continuing to develop the skills necessary to play matches.

Junior Beginners (F1 +)

These classes take place on a full size court with low compression balls (mini tennis green). Lessons are dynamic with numerous games while teaching children the proper technique for all the main strokes in tennis – forehand, backhand, serve & volleys.

Each class lasts one hour and the ‘Junior Beginners’ class is suitable for children in F1 upwards, who are learning the initial skills.

Junior Improvers (F1 +)

These classes take place on a full size court with low compression balls (mini tennis green). Lessons are dynamic with numerous games while teaching children the proper technique for all the main strokes in tennis – forehand, backhand, serve & volleys.

Each class lasts one hour and the ‘Junior Improvers’ class is suitable for children in F1 upwards, who have been to classes before and are continuing to develop the skills necessary to play matches.

Junior Advanced (F1 +)

These classes take place on a full size court with proper balls. Players are invited to join an advanced class once they have mastered all the strokes. Players will progress to this class from ‘Mid Tennis Advanced’ once they are old enough.

Holiday Camps

Totally Tennis Camps

Our ‘Totally Tennis Camps,’ are suitable for children aged 5 - 14 years old. They provide three hours of professional tennis coaching each day and typically run from Monday to Friday each week during the school holidays.

The aim is to give young children plenty of time on court and help them develop agility, balance, coordination and speed. These skills will benefit them in every sport they play. The camps also let them enjoy their holidays by having fun playing sport and making new friends in a safe environment.

In addition to sound teaching of all the strokes in tennis (forehand, backhand, volley & serve) children will participate in games, drills and exercises aimed at developing their general sporting skills and interest in tennis. The wide variety of tennis based games and exercises makes tennis fun so that children enjoy every minute.

At all our camps children are supervised at all times and are kept active for almost all of the three hours. The time passes incredibly quickly for all concerned and children are usually be quite tired afterwards - so don't expect them to be too active at home in the evenings.

Multi Sport Camps

Our ‘Multi Sports Camps,’ which are suitable for children aged 5 - 14 years old, provide three hours of professional sport tuition each day and typically run from Monday to Friday each week.

The aim is to help young children develop agility, balance, coordination and speed, skills that will benefit them in every sport they play as well as letting them have fun playing sport and making new friends during the school holidays. In addition to a daily tennis lesson these camps also incorporate other sports including as football, cricket, hockey, baseball, tag rugby, American football etc.

At all our camps children are supervised at all times and are kept active for almost all of the three hours, which seem to pass incredibly quickly for all concerned. Children will usually be quite tired afterwards so don't expect them to be too active at home in the evenings.

Adult Classes

Adult Beginners

Our ‘adult beginners’ classes combine fun games and exercises with learning the proper technique for the main strokes in tennis – serve, forehand, backhand & volley.

Lessons are in small groups (max eight) and everyone has a great time.

Adult Improvers

Our ‘adult improver’ classes combine fun games and exercises with developing proper technique for the main strokes in tennis – serve, forehand, backhand & volley.

Come along and join the fun and let Queen Elsa help us give your children an interest in tennis.

They also include tactics, doubles positioning etc. to help you take your tennis to the next level. Lessons are in small groups (max eight) and everyone has a great time.
