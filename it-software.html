Pro Coach IT Software

The ‘Group-Lessons.com’ website works with ‘ProCoachIT’ offline software to streamline the administration required for a Group-Lessons programme. Initially built and tested, over a twenty-year period, by a large tennis coaching business, its functions were designed to be generic for any Group-Lessons provider.

Group-Lessons are economical for participants and can be very lucrative for instructors as a small amount from each person adds up to a large income per class. However while ‘one to one’ lessons are simple to arrange requiring just a place and a time (see 1-2-1Lessons.com) Group-Lessons are much more complicated requiring all participants to be of a similar age and/or ability level.

Our software allows you to set up classes, at numerous facilities if necessary, with your own specified ‘class types’ for each age group and ability level you cater for. It then allows you to enrol people on these classes building a database of customers in the process.

Data can also be added about your instructors, local schools where you promote your courses etc. and these databases are interrelated so you can easily extract details such as class lists, mailing lists, customer retention figures, income details, staff attendance etc. (See www.ProCoachIT.com for full details).

If you operate Group-Lessons this software will change your life. It is user friendly and intuitive.
