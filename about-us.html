ABOUT US

Group-Lessons.com allows providers of Group-Lessons, in any activity, to provide general information about their business, upload details of their courses (times, dates etc.) and accept online bookings.

Most businesses see a marked improvement in sales when they give customers the opportunity to book and pay online. To add this facility to your business simply complete the following steps

1. Click ‘Register as a group lessons provider’ above. On registering you will get three months free trial of our ‘Group-Lessons’ software.

2. Choose a service provider to accept your credit card payments online. If you only take a small amount of credit card payments, or are taking online payments for the first time, you can set up a PayPal account. They charge more per transaction but do not charge a set up or monthly fee. If you will be taking a lot of online payments you should set up a ‘merchant account’ with CARDSAVE.
